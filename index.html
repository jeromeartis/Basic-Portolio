Jerome Artis is an enthusiastic, charismatic individual who was born in Brooklyn New York. Compelled to make his parent proud and make his own path, he joined the United States Navy. He severed for twelve honorable years, while starting his own family and attending school full time in San Diego, California and Atlanta, Georgia. His first start in technology came a few months after getting out of the military. He was offered a level II position as User System Support Specialist where he provides deskside/PBX support to end users.
